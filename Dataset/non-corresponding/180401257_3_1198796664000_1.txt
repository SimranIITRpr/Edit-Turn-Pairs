<edit_text>Plot
Around Christmas time in Gotham City, the wealthy aristocratic Cobblepots drop their infant deformed infant baby in the sewers, abandoning him of his ridiculed look. Eventually he's raised by emperor penguins amongst an abandoned zoo. Thirty-three years later, it is Christmas time again as the city is being run by Mayor Hamilton Hill, who deals with the ambitious but ruthless business tycoon Max Shreck. Gotham becomes under attack by the &quot;Red Triangle Circus Gang&quot; in a recent ceremony, although the heist is later dis<strong><strike>pu</strike></strong><strong>rup</strong>ted by Batman.
