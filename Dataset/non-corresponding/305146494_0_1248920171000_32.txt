Early life
Born in , Bonds grew up in and attended Junípero Serra High School in and excelled in baseball, basketball and football. As a freshman, he spent the baseball season on the JV team. The next three years—1980 to 1982—he starred on the varsity team. He batted for a .467&amp;nbsp;batting average his senior year, and was honored as a prep All-American.&lt;ref name=MLB&gt;&lt;/ref&gt; The Giants drafted Bonds in the second round of the 1982 MLB draft as a high school senior,&lt;ref name=br-stats&gt;&lt;/ref&gt; but the Giants and Bonds were unable to agree on contract terms, so Bonds instead decided to attend college.&lt;ref&gt;&lt;/ref&gt;</edit_text>
